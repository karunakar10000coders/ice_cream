Our Journey

Born From Passion, Churned With Love

Scoop Paradise was founded in 2021 by a group of culinary enthusiasts who wanted to bring back the nostalgic experience of local scoop shops, but with an uncompromising gourmet twist.

We set out to create ice cream that is completely natural, highlighting local seasonal produce. Every scoop tells a story of local collaborations—from honey harvested by nearby beekeepers to vanilla pods sourced ethically from sustainable growers.
